SPRING Redemption Policy — Key Constraints

  • Quarterly cap: Maximum 2.5% of the Fund's total outstanding shares per quarter
  • Annual effective max: ~10% of your position per year (4 quarters × 2.5%)
  • Board discretion: The Board of Trustees can modify, suspend, or terminate the redemption plan entirely at any time
  • No obligation: The Fund is not obligated to redeem any shares — approval is at the Board's discretion
  • Monthly subscriptions: You can invest monthly, but redemptions are quarterly only
  • Long-term hold: Full exit from a large position requires multiple years of quarterly redemptions
